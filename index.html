Healthcare Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

Insurance Policy

The Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana intends to give accident insurance coverage to Indian citizens between the ages of 18 and 70. In 2016, it was observed that only 20% of the Indian citizens had an insurance cover. However, this scheme aspires to change this statistic in a positive manner.

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana

Policy for Labour Sector

The Ministry of Labour and Employment is in charge of providing such policies to unorganised sector workers and their families (up to 5 people). The beneficiary under this scheme is entitled to hospitalisation expenses up to Rs. 30,000 for illnesses that require hospitalisation.

Universal Health Insurance Scheme (UHIS)

Medical Coverage

The Universal Health Insurance Scheme is introduced by four public insurance companies to provide health insurance cover to needy families. This Scheme offers individual as well as group health insurance. It covers hospitalization, accident, and disability.
